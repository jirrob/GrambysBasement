Digital Sequence Description

Press the blue splitter on the back to activate the sequence. This was specifically designed for a sequence rocket pod, so there may be some issues trying to install it on something else.

Rocket Pod Description

Press the blue splitter on the back to fire it. It could be taken off the base by the stick and placed onto something else. You could also try taking the stick off the rocket pod by grabbing the connector ball from the front
